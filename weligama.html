Your Journey Starts Here

Weligama, situated along Sri Lanka's southern coast, is a charming coastal town known for its idyllic beaches and vibrant surfing culture. The name "Weligama" translates to "sandy village," accurately depicting its scenic shoreline adorned with palm trees and golden sands. This tranquil town has gained popularity as a surfing haven, offering gentle waves suitable for both beginners and experienced surfers.

The town's picturesque bay is dotted with colorful fishing boats, providing a glimpse into the local fishing industry and offering opportunities for boat rides and whale watching excursions. Weligama's relaxed atmosphere, coupled with its friendly locals, makes it a welcoming destination for travelers seeking a laid-back beach escape

Weligama's central location also makes it convenient for exploring nearby attractions such as the historic Galle Fort, stunning Mirissa Beach, and the famous stilt fishermen of Koggala. Overall, Weligama offers a serene coastal getaway and a chance to immerse oneself in the beauty of Sri Lanka's southern shores.
