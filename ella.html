Your Journey Starts Here

Ella is a charming hillside town situated in the central highlands of Sri Lanka. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, cool climate, and vibrant atmosphere, Ella has become a popular destination for travellers seeking natural beauty and outdoor adventures.

Surrounded by lush tea plantations, rolling hills, and scenic viewpoints, Ella offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and mountains. The town is famous for its iconic viewpoint, Ella Rock, which can be reached through a rewarding hike, providing a bird's-eye view of the landscape below.

Ella is also known for its picturesque waterfalls, including the impressive Ravana Falls. Visitors can enjoy treks to these waterfalls and refresh themselves in the cool, crystal-clear waters. Additionally, the Nine Arch Bridge, a historic railway bridge nestled amidst the greenery, is another popular attraction that showcases the region's colonial-era architecture.

The town itself offers a laid-back ambience with a range of accommodation options, from boutique guesthouses to eco-friendly lodges. The local market, eateries, and cafes provide a taste of Sri Lankan cuisine and hospitality. Overall, Ella captures the essence of Sri Lanka's natural beauty and offers a peaceful escape for travellers seeking relaxation and exploration in the hills.
