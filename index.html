Welcome to

SRI LANKA

Welcome to Sri Lanka, a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean. Immerse yourself in a rich tapestry of history, from ancient cities like Anuradhapura to the colonial charm of Galle. Explore lush rainforests, misty hills, and pristine beaches, including the stunning tea plantations of Nuwara Eliya and the diverse wildlife of national parks like Yala. Indulge in aromatic spices and unique flavours through traditional dishes, from rice and curry to hoppers. Whether you seek adventure or relaxation, Sri Lanka offers both in abundance, all while experiencing the warmth of its culture and the friendliness of its people.

Gems in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka boasts a dazzling array of precious gems, including sapphires, rubies, and emeralds, renowned for their vibrant colours and exceptional quality. With a history of gem mining, the country is a global hub for these treasures, drawing enthusiasts and traders.

Cultural foods

Sri Lankan cuisine revolves around aromatic curries, often fiery, served with rice and coconut. Don't miss spicy sambals, fresh seafood, and unique street food like hoppers and kottu roti. Explore local eateries for an authentic taste of Sri Lanka's diverse culinary delights.

Hand Craft

Sri Lanka's rich handcraft heritage includes intricate batik art, traditional wood carving, and exquisite mask-making. Skilled artisans create unique pieces that reflect the country's cultural diversity and history. Crafts, with intricate designs and cultural significance, are often generational.
